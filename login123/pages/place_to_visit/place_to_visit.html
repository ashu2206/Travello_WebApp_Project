Popular Cities in India

Dubai Skylines

Based on the average height of the ten tallest buildings, Dubai has the tallest skyline in the world. As of 2012, the skyline of Dubai is ranked eighth in the world with 248 buildings rising in height.

Agra

It is famous for its Mughal architecture and for being home to one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal - a monument of incredible elegance and architectural perfection.

Wall of China

In a land of modern cities and towering skyscrapers, the Great Wall of China, built between the 14th and 17th centuries, is a stark contrast but a striking image that all visitors to China should see.

Bengaluru

Apart from Cubbon Park in the heart of the city skirting several of its iconic landmark its like a place of peace such as the State Central Library , For any kind of shopping , the area in and around MG Road is your mecca .

The Louvre,Paris

there is one museum in the whole world that you absolutely must see in your life, it's the Louvre. Even if you are not a fan of museums, this one is worth the trip to Paris to see. Although people know it as home to most famous painting in world.

Mathura

Mathura is one of the most famous pilgrimage centers in India and marks the birthplace of the ancient Hindu deity, Lord Krishna. The town is one of the oldest in India and extremely popular for its ancient culture and tradition.
